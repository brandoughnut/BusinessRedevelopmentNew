ABOUT US

Komachi strives to translate the beauty of Lodi into appetizing and authentic Japanese cuisine. We promise to be your new sushi family. Welcome home!

Established in 2013. Sunny has been in Japanese food industry for 25 years. She has launched a brand new restaurant in Lodi to serve the people she fell so in love with.

Now days, there are very little Japanese Restaurants who specialize in Authentic Japanese Cuisine. Well, look no further! We specialize in Authentic Japanese Cuisine to modern sushi rolls as well. Please come in, let us serve you. You'll feel right at home.
